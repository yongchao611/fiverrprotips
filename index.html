Yes! Creating a Fiverr account and posting gigs is completely free. Fiverr takes a 20% commission on each order you complete, so you only pay when you earn. There are no monthly fees or listing charges.

Getting your first order requires a combination of a well-optimized gig (compelling title, detailed description, eye-catching images), competitive pricing for beginners, and patience. We recommend starting with a lower price point, promoting your gig on social media, and delivering exceptional work to earn those crucial first 5-star reviews.

Fiverr's search algorithm ranks gigs based on relevance, seller performance (reviews, response rate, order completion rate), and gig quality. Key ranking factors include your gig title keywords, tags, description content, conversion rate, and overall seller metrics. Optimizing each of these elements is crucial for visibility.

Earnings on Fiverr vary widely depending on your skill, niche, pricing, and effort. Beginners might earn $100–$500/month, while experienced sellers in high-demand niches can earn $5,000–$20,000+/month. Top-rated and Pro sellers with premium services can earn even more. The key is to start, learn, and consistently improve.
